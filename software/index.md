# Software

Our group develops and maintains several open-source software packages, primarily in the [Julia programming language](https://julialang.org/).

## Demographics
~~~
<div class="card-grid">
  <div class="card">
    <h3><a href="https://github.com/ExoplanetsSysSim">ExoplanetsSysSim.jl</a></h3>
    <p>Forward modeling framework for exoplanet demographics. Used to characterize the architectures of planetary systems from Kepler data.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3><a href="https://github.com/ExoJulia/SysSimExClusters">SysSimExClusters</a></h3>
    <p>Draw samples of planetary systems consistent with Kepler observations.</p>
  </div>
</div>
~~~
## Extremely Precise Radial Velocities
~~~
<div class="card-grid">
  <div class="card">
    <h3><a href="https://github.com/palumbom/GRASS">GRASS</a></h3>
    <p>Granulation and Stellar Spectra Simulator. Simulates the effects of solar/stellar granulation on radial velocity measurements.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <h3><a href="https://github.com/christiangil/StellarSpectraObservationFitting.jl">StellarSpectraObservationFitting.jl</a></h3>
    <p>Data-driven modeling of telluric features and stellar variability in high-resolution spectra.</p>
  </div>
<div class="card">
    <h3><a href="https://github.com/christiangil/SSOFApplication">SSOFApplication.jl</a></h3>
    <p>Examples applying StellarSpectraObservationFitting.jl (SSOF) to NEID observations.</p>
  </div>
</div>
~~~
